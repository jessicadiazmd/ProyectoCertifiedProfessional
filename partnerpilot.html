Partner pilot

We are looking for dynamic and successful companies with an innovative and sustainable approach to business! Become a pilot partner for our certification programme and get exclusive insights into the first European-wide standard to certify cross-functional competencies. Discuss and engage in a network of European experts and pilot partners the requirements for the future workforce and life-long learning.

Become our pilot partner and enjoy the following benefits:

- Free and facilitated certification of your staff
- Be among the first to test and use this state
- Join an influential EU network of renowned academia and business
- Demonstrate your pro-active attitude towards HR management and innovation
- Become a member of our expert pool and feedback on the future development of Certified Professional
- Receive suggestions for and admittance to high level training modules to strengthen competencies of your staff

What we expect from pilot partners:

- Certification of selected staff (5 to 20 candidates)
- Feedback on the value and applicability of Certified Professional from your institution’s perspective
- Consent to us using collaboration as reference in communication measures for our public launch
